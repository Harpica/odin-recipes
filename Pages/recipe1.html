Rolls with cheese and greens

Ingredients

  • Pita bread: 4 sheets
  • Butter: 180 g
  • Soft brynza: 500 g
  • Garlic: 5-6 cloves
  • Fresh herbs: basil, cilantro, dill, parsley

Grind room temperature butter and other ingredients until smooth in a blender. Spread the pita bread sheets with the mixture, roll the pita bread into the tightest possible roll, roll it up in foil and put it in the refrigerator until the oil hardens. After the butter has set, cut the roll into pieces. If desired, you can add all sorts of nuts like cedar or walnuts to the greens.
