Banana bread

Ingredients

  • Bananas: 3 pieces
  • Vegetable oil: 80 ml
  • Sugar: 100 g
  • Flour: 200 g
  • Raw eggs: 2
  • Baking powder: 3 tsp

Set the oven to 180 degrees. While it is heating, we knead the bananas with a fork, then we just put everything else there and mix it, you can use a fork, you can use a blender. Bake 55 minutes.
