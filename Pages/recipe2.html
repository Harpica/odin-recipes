Khachapuri

Ingredients

  • Kefir: 250 ml (one glass)
  • Butter: 100 g
  • Flour: 10-12 tbs
  • Slaked soda: 1/4 tsp
  • Spicy cheese (brynza or ossetian) for filling: 400 g
  • Raw egg for filling: 2

Melt butter, mix with flour, knead until the dough sticks out of your hands. Stir slaked soda with dough. After that put the dough into refrigerator (do not forget to cover it with smth). Put the dough on a baking sheet with baking paper put the stuffing on top. Bake for 35-40 min for 180 degrees.
